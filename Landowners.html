Farmers

Worried about the next crop season? Don't be. Most farmers we encounter have land that is not suitable for crops. Why not turn your unsuitable land into residual income? S.E.P. can align your property with the nation's leading solar developers and negotiate a 20-year lease on your behalf. S.E.P. can also offer an outright purchase of the property if preferred. Along with a 20-year lease, there are also tax advantages and increases for inflation factored into some leases. Submit your Site for a Preliminary Site Analysis and discover the advantages of leasing your property for solar development.

Landowners

Do you own a piece of property that was left to you? Or do you own a piece of land that you're not sure what to do with? Stop paying taxes and start earning a residual income with a solar lease. S.E.P. has been able to assist numerous landowners in achieving financial gains through solar lease or purchase arrangements.

Industrial Landowners

Have a piece of Industrial or Commercial property that needs a tenant? How about leasing the land for solar? A long term solar lease could stabilize less advantageous properties and create a residual revenue stream. Properties with environmental and other issues work well with solar developments. Submit your site and lets see if a solar lease works for you.

S.E.P. Purchase Program

There is never a reason to put your money at risk, let S.E.P. purchase the desired property. S.E.P has the ability to secure specific properties and give the developers the time required to complete all Interconnection and local permits. SEP will then be the lessor or assign all the purchase rights to the developer for a fee.
